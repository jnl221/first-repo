My first dataset is the MTA permanent art catalog As a big fan of the NYC subway, I'm particularly interested in attempts to make public spaces beautiful.

  1. How is this data organized and collected? I found this dataset on Jeremy Singer-Vine's list of datasets, and it's hosted on data.gov, under State of New York. MTA Arts and Design, which runs the public arts program, collects the data which "provides the branch or station and the artist and artwork information." The data also has the year the art was created, a description of each piece and a link to the image.
